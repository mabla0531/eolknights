# Home

Welcome to the Epiphany of Our Lord Council of the Knights of Columbus.
Here you will find information about our current activities
and events as well as information on our council.

<br>

We have a public access calendar to help you plan to join us in upcoming events.
If you would like to join us, please visit our membership page.

<br>

The Knights of Columbus is a Men's Catholic Organization based on
the principles of Charity, Unity, Fraternity, and Patriotism.
We support the Catholic Church, Priests and each other through these principles.
To read more about the Knights of Columbus, please visit the
Supreme Council Website.
